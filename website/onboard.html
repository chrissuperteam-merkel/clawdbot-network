❓ FAQ

What does the app do?

It routes web traffic from AI agents through your phone's internet connection. You share bandwidth, you earn SOL.

Is it safe?

Yes. Traffic passes through your connection but nothing is stored on your device. You can stop anytime.

Does it use a lot of battery?

No. The proxy service is lightweight — expect less than 5% extra battery drain per day.

Do I need root?

No. Works on any unmodified Android 8.0+ device.

When do I get paid?

Earnings are calculated per session. SOL payments will be sent directly to your wallet once on-chain payments are live.

Can I use my daily phone?

Yes, but a spare phone on WiFi is ideal — always connected, always earning.
